Pfeiffer Purple Sand Beach, California

Why Should I Go There?

This beautiful, secluded beach is located on the Big Sur coast of California. The purple sand is caused by manganese garnet in the nearby cliffs being eroded away. The best time to view the purple is directly after rain or storms. Unfortunately, swimming is discouraged due to many rocks and strong currents. However, it is a beautiful place for a walk or a picnic and is even dog friendly! The breathtaking views are definitely worth visiting.

Activities In The Area

There are many restaurants located near Pfeiffer beach, including Big Sur Roadhouse, Fernwood Bar and Grill, and Big Sur Bakery. Also located nearby is the Pfeiffer Big Sur campground and state park. Some other fun activities near the beach include horseback riding, hiking along waterfalls, or visiting a hot springs.
